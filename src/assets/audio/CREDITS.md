# Audio credits

All clips in this folder are from Kenney (kenney.nl), released under
[CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) — public domain,
no attribution required. Kept here anyway for provenance.

Source packs (trimmed to the files actually used by [`../../audio.ts`](../../audio.ts)):

- [Sci-Fi Sounds](https://kenney.nl/assets/sci-fi-sounds) — doors, engines, lasers, force fields, computer noise, metal impacts
- [Impact Sounds](https://kenney.nl/assets/impact-sounds) — mining/recycler impacts
- [Interface Sounds](https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds) — UI clicks, glitches, confirmation
- [Digital Audio](https://kenney.nl/assets/digital-audio) — tones, power-up/down, zaps
- [RPG Audio](https://kenney.nl/assets/rpg-audio) — creaks, footsteps, coin/cloth handling, metal latch/pot

Kenney doesn't publish a dedicated long-form ambience/drone pack, so room beds
(hydroponics "water", cantina "crowd", etc.) are built from the closest-fitting
loopable texture (force fields, low engine drones) rather than a literal
recording of that room — noted per-room in `audio.ts`.
